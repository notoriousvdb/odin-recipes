Spagetti

Description

This is spagetti.

Ingredients

  • 12 ounces of dried spagetti
  • 4 quarts water
  • 1 tablespoon salt

Cooking Instructions

  1. In pot, bring water to a boil
  2. Cook spagetti to suggested al dente time
  3. Drain spagetti, reserviing 1 cup of water for sauce
  4. Add cooked spagetti to sauce
